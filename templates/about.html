About

This budget voting system was created to encourage member participation in our hackerspace. It's an experiment in decentralized decision-making and community funding.

How It Works

  • Any member can submit a proposal for equipment or resources
  • Members vote on proposals - minimum approval threshold varies by proposal type:
    • Basic supplies: 5% of members required
    • Proposals over €50: 20% of members required
    • Other proposals: 10% of members required
  • Approved proposals are automatically funded if within available budget, and a notification is sent to our Telegram group
  • The member that submitted proposal is given go ahead to execute purchase & claim reimbursement via the usual channels
  • If item is not purchased within 30 days members can vote to cancel the approval

Funding

The initial budget of €300 was funded by the Mercadillo from Jairo's donations. Currently, the Association contributes €50 per month to keep the budget alive.

Features & Governance

Any member is welcome to suggest changes at https://github.com/makespacemadrid/manavote, which will be voted at our next monthly meeting.
